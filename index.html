How it works

REDACTR is a web application that enables you to safely share your written content on social media by scrambling portions of the texts you don't want the world to see.
STEP 1: Write or paste your content into the first form field. STEP 2: Write out the corresponding word you which to scramble in the next form field separated by spaces in the case of more than one word. STEP 3: Input the character for the replacement of words you want to scramble in the last field. The default value for this field is asterik(*) therefore the field is optional. STEP 4: Hit "REDACT NOW" button to scramble the specified word(s) in your content. "RETURN" button returns your content to it's original form. "CLEAR" button clears all the values in each field. "COPY" button will copy your scrambled content into your clipboard. "SHARE" button will share your content to any social media of your choice.
